What is a web developer?

Specializes in the development of World Wide Web applications using client–server model.

- Front-end designs interface and user experience
- Back-end develops the behaviors, functions and data
- Full-stack understands and builds and connects two fields.

  • What are website developers' works?

    A front end developer has one general responsibility: bring users to the website, keep them there and let them digest the contents.

    Common tasks:

    Optimizing the user experience. Using HTML, JavaScript and CSS to bring concepts to life. Developing and maintaining the user interface. Implementing design on many types of device and browser. Following SEO best practices.

  • How rich are they?

    Average frontend developer salary is $75,195 according to PayScale

    Germany: $60,000 Switzerland: $93,667 Denmark: $62,503 Vietnam

  • How many are they?

    27.42% of programmer stated they are front-end developer in Stack Overflow survey

    In October 2018, Glassdoor advertised 14,157 front-end developer jobs in the USA, 6,350 jobs in the UK, and 1,070 in Canada.

    #1 21 October 2021

    How high full-stack salary compare to front and back?

    - According to estimates from Glassdoor, Indeed, and ZipRecruiter, it is between $105,813 and $108,984 per year in US.
    - In Vietnam, a full-stack programmer's salary can reach more than 1,400 USD.
    - Usually a little higher than front-end and back-end.

    #2 21 October 2021

    What is full stack development?

    - Full stack development is the process of working on a project single-handed, cover client and server side

    #3 21 October 2021

    Why do we need a full stack, but not a pair of front and back?

    • Reduce the need and cost of people
    • Complete the overall design faster, better team communication
    • Flexibility to switch between front and back end development

    #4 24 September 2021

    How to be a full-stack developer?

    Learn both front end and back end.
